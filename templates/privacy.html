Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use Sonico, we also store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Sonico service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and breakdowns, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the application.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your authenticated account. We apply standard security practices to protect stored information. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us or using the account settings.

4. Third Party Services

Sonico does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use minimal infrastructure services (such as hosting providers) to operate the application, but these providers are not permitted to access or use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently. You can contact us via the support email listed on the application's help page.
